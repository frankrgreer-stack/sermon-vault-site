More voices for the vault

Thirteen seats filled, and the pulpit still has room. Worth a wing whenever you're ready:

  • Steven Furtick — Elevation Church; the biggest sermon channel on YouTube today
  • Max Lucado — pastor-author of Anxious for Nothing; gentle as a back porch
  • Priscilla Shirer — Bible teacher (and Tony Evans's daughter); Fervent, War Room
  • David Jeremiah — Turning Point; fifty years of steady radio exposition
  • John MacArthur — Grace Community; verse-by-verse for the deep-study wing
  • Robert Schuller — Hour of Power from the Crystal Cathedral; Osteen's forerunner
  • Chuck Swindoll — Insight for Living; the storyteller's storyteller
  • Billy Sunday & Aimee Semple McPherson — the tent-revival era; a historic audio wing
