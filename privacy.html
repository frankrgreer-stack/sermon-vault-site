Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Sermon Vault is a curated library of publicly available sermon videos. We respect your privacy and keep data collection to the absolute minimum.

What we collect

Nothing, directly. Sermon Vault has no account system, no analytics, and no servers of our own. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Purchases

The optional Collection Plate is a tip jar that uses Apple’s In-App Purchase. Payments are processed entirely by Apple — we never see your card details or personal data. Giving is always optional and is never required to use any part of the app.

Third-party content

The app displays sermon videos hosted on YouTube and loads typography from Google Fonts. When a player loads or you tap “Watch on YouTube,” Google/YouTube may receive your IP address and set cookies under Google’s own privacy policy, as on any site embedding YouTube. We have no access to that data.

Google Privacy Policy — policies.google.com/privacy
YouTube Terms — youtube.com/t/terms

Children

Sermon Vault does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date above will reflect any change.
