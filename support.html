Frequently asked

Is Sermon Vault free?

Yes. The whole library is free. The optional Collection Plate lets you give if the app has fed you — and every penny of profit goes to charitable causes around the world.

Why won’t a video play?

Sermons stream from YouTube, so a connection is required. If a video won’t load, check your internet connection, or tap “Watch on YouTube” to open it directly.

How does giving work?

The Collection Plate uses Apple’s secure In-App Purchase. Choose a gift amount; Apple handles payment. Giving is always optional and never required to use the app.

Do you collect my data?

No. Sermon Vault has no accounts, analytics, or servers of its own. See our Privacy Policy.
